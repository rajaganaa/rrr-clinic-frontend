About this app
How the AI works›
The Antahkarana AI engine processes your question through 7 stages inspired by Vedantic cognitive architecture — Manas (understanding), Chitta (memory retrieval), Buddhi (reasoning), Ahamkara (confidence scoring), and Sakshi (verification). Built by Rajaganapathy M, AI Engineer, SRM University.
Safety & Disclaimer›
This AI assistant provides general medical information only. Always consult Dr. Rajeswari or a qualified medical professional before taking any medication. Do not rely solely on AI for medical decisions.
