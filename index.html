About this app
How the AI works ›
The Antahkarana AI engine processes your question through 4 stages — Manas (understanding), Chitta (memory retrieval), Buddhi (reasoning), and Sakshi (verification). Vision AI reads medicine strips, lab reports, and X-rays. A Tanglish summary is generated for every response so village patients can understand easily.
Safety & Disclaimer ›
This AI assistant provides general medical information only. Always consult Dr. Rajeswari or a qualified medical professional before taking any medication. Do not rely solely on AI for medical decisions.
Technology ›
Frontend: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS PWA. Backend: Python FastAPI on Azure. AI: GPT-4o Vision + Claude Sonnet for Tanglish summaries. Voice: Web Speech API (Tamil + English). Storage: ChromaDB vector store.
